Short Bio

    Marçal Rossinyol received his B.Sc., M.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Sciences from the Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona, Barcelona, Spain, in 2004, 2006, and 2009 respectively. Until 2020, he was an associated researcher at the Computer Vision Center where he participated in more than 20 Competitive Research projects, both nationally and from the European Commission, and in more than 15 technology transfer projects, being the IP of several of them.

    In 2012 and 2014 he was a Marie Curie researcher at the French company Itesoft and at the Laboratoire Informatique, Image et Interaction of the Université de La Rochelle.

    He has been a Teaching Assistant and an Adjunct Lecturer at the Computer Science Department of the Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona since 2005. He has received the accreditations for Lecturer and Tenured Assistant from AQU in 2010 and 2017 respectively and the Maître de Conférence accreditation from the Education Ministry from France.

    He has served as program committee member in a dozen of international conferences and has been the organizer of several international workshops, competitions and tutorials. He is co-author of more than 95 publications in scientific journals and international conferences, with more than 2,150 citations, has an h-index of 26.

    He has participated in several entrepreneurship programs within the fields of digital humanities and machine reading systems. In 2019 he co-founded the spin-off company AllRead where he currently is the CTO.
